American style coffee in the tourist surfing town of Santa Teresa. Semi-authentic Costa Rican feel, within a stones throw from the jungle and the beach.
A few parking spots out front.

Great american style breakfast, fantastic cappucinos, very friendly staff. Overall great experience.

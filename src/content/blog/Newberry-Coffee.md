Newberry is a small coffee roaster and cafe in Torrance. Huge open parking lot on Saturday morning. Small selection of single origin coffees.
Roast date is not mentioned on the coffee, just a best before date - labelled a few months out. Neat poster-boards with coffee flavour information.

![Coffee Corner](../../assets/NEWBERRY-COFFEE_03-07-2026_2.JPG)

Not much space inside or outside to enjoy your drink. More of a coffee-to-go spot.
